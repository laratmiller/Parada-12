HAPPIER THAN EVER

Happier Than Ever, The World Tour is the ongoing sixth concert tour by American singer Billie Eilish, in support of her second studio album Happier Than Ever (2021).

The tour commenced on February 3, 2022, in New Orleans, Louisiana at Smoothie King Center and is set to conclude on September 30, 2022, in Perth, Australia at RAC Arena.
